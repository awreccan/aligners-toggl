RReminders (same for everyone)

One-time: open the Reminders app → Add List → name it exactly Aligners.

In “Aligners Off”, after the URL action, add two nudges. For each: an Adjust Date (Current Date, Add, 45 min then 90 min) → Add New Reminder (List = Aligners, Alert = the adjusted date):

+45 min “Put your aligners back in 🦷”
+90 min “Aligners out 90 min — wrap it up 🦷”

These count from the moment you take them out — a “you’ve been out a while” nudge. Your true remaining daily out-budget is the ring in the app.

In “Aligners On”, after its URL action: Find Reminders (List = Aligners, Is Completed = off) → Remove Reminders — this clears any pending nudges.
