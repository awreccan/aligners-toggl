Connect Toggl

“22” logs every time you take your aligners out as a Toggl Track time entry — so your wear-time lives in the same place as the rest of your day. Because Toggl’s API can’t be called from a browser, a tiny Google Apps Script you own does it for you. One-time setup.

  1. Deploy the backend Apps Script (holds your Toggl token) and copy its Web-app /exec URL. Full recipe in backend/DEPLOY.md — ~5 min, paste the token into Script Properties, deploy “Execute as Me / Anyone”.
  2. Paste that URL here:
Setting this up for Jason?

Jason runs his own copy: he deploys the same backend/Code.gs under his Google account with his Toggl token in Script Properties, then pastes his /exec URL above. His token never touches this app — only his proxy URL does. (The Settings gear ⚙ re-opens this screen anytime.)

Only the /exec URL is stored on this device — never your Toggl token. The URL is an unguessable secret; revoke it anytime by deleting the deployment.
